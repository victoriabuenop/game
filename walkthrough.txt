Game description:
- Princess Peach has lost her crown... Find 3 crown Items to help her get it back!!!

To win:
- Pick up 3 crown objects from the ground by walking over them without being killed by the enemies (2 ghosts)

To lose:
- Princess Peach has only 3 lifes that are displayed at the right top of the screen. Let ghost touch you to
lose 1 life. Make it happend 3 times to lose the game.

Good Luck!!!!
